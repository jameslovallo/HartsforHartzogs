Kaitlyn & Andrew

Welcome to our Website!

Thank you so much for visiting our wedding website! We are so excited to celebrate our special day with our family and friends. This website contains wedding day details and much more - check back for updates!

Our Story

Chapter 1Andrew and Kaitlyn met in 2014 through their mutual friend, Kaylynn. Kaylynn invited some friends to go out to dinner for her birthday; but only Andrew and Kaitlyn were able to go. According to Andrew, he wanted to ask Kaitlyn on a date after dinner but he could not since she was seeing someone else.

Chapter 2Flash forward to August 2016, at Kaylynn's wedding. Andrew and Kaitlyn met again; and then proceeded to talk and dance during the whole reception. Kaitlyn mentioned that she was in need of volunteer coaches for a youth soccer program she helped oversee in Detroit. A couple of days later, Andrew signed up. This then began a three month process of Andrew asking Kaitlyn. In December 2016, Kaitlyn finally agreed. (Finally!)

Chapter 3Andrew and Kaitlyn proceeded to grow in Christ together, learn about one another, and support each other in their individual goals. The Lord began to reveal to them that spending their love together was in His plan through going to church together, doing devotionals together, and praying together. During this time, Andrew got baptized at their church (Mack Avenue Community Church in Detroit).

Chapter 4On February 25, 2018; Andrew asked Kaitlyn to marry him. Of course, she said yes! Now, as they prepare to start the life together in September 2018, Kaitlyn works in foster care and will begin studying for her Clinical Master's in Social Work; and Andrew is about to finish tech school and is looking to start his career in heating and cooling. They plan to live on the Eastside of Detroit where their church is located.
